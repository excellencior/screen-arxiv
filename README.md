# Screen Arxiv 🎬

**Your Personal Archive for Movies and TV Series.**

Screen Arxiv is a premium, Notion-inspired media tracking application designed for those who want a clean, aesthetic, and distraction-free way to archive their cinematic journey. 

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎞️ Movie Tracking
Keep a complete record of every film you've watched or plan to see. 
- **Detailed Modals**: Dive into cast lists, runtimes, and movie summaries.
- **Trailer Integration**: Watch trailers with a single click.
- **Intuitive Status**: Quickly mark movies as *Watched*, *Watching*, *Will Watch*, or *On Hold*.

### 📺 TV Series & Episodes
Track your progress through every season and episode with precision.
- **Season Management**: Beautifully organized season lists with individual posters.
- **Episode Tracking**: Mark individual episodes as watched to auto-calculate your overall series progress.
- **Quick Actions**: Mark entire seasons or the whole series as watched instantly.

### 📊 Insights & Analytics
Visualize your viewing habits through a clean, data-driven dashboard.
- **Library Overview**: See your total movies, series, and episode counts at a glance.
- **Decade Distribution**: Discover which eras of cinema you explore the most.
- **Genre Breakdown**: Identify your favorite genres through visual charts.
- **Progress Metrics**: See your series completion rates and watching trends.

### 🔍 Integrated Search
Powered by TMDB, find any movie or show in seconds.
- **Quick Add**: See something you like? Add it to your library instantly.
- **Rich Metadata**: Automatically pulls in high-quality posters, years, and runtimes.

### 🎨 Premium Experience
- **Notion Aesthetic**: A minimal, monospace-driven design featuring the *JetBrains Mono* font.
- **Adaptive Theme**: Seamless Light and Dark modes that remember your preference.
- **Responsive Layout**: Designed to look stunning on desktops and mobile devices alike.
- **Smooth Motion**: Fluid, spring-based animations for every interaction.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **React** & **Vite** for a fast, responsive interface.
- **Bootstrap** & **Vanilla CSS** for the clean architectural styling.
- **Framer Motion** for premium interactions and animations.
- **Lucide React** for consistent, meaningful iconography.
- **TMDB API** for a comprehensive global media database.
